Pizza Places

My favorite pizzerias in Venice

Antico Forno

Antico Forno makes with no doubts the best pizza in Venice, and is even recognized by some as one of the top 10 pizza places in Italy! The incredible knowledge for this art, the multiple types of doughs, the choice of excellent Italian ingredients, the dedication and care in the making, the number of varieties and the daily amount served are simply impressive.

Address:

Rugheta del Ravano, 973 Near Rialto Bridge, 30125 Venice Italy

What I like about it

A light bite pizza place that sells it by the slice. Great taste, though very much more like a pizza sandwich than a thin Italian slice. They have the pizzas out and you pick a slice to b eat. They oven it and serve. Off you go.

Farini

Chiesa di San Lio is a great attraction for tourists who come to eat at this cafe after long walks. Discover new dishes of Italian cuisine in the comfort of Farini. The recipe for success of this place is its perfectly cooked pizza Margherita, focaccia and pizza salads. Great cappuccino, espresso or latte are worth a try here.

Address:

Sestiere Castello 5602 C/o campo San Lio, 30125 Venice Italy

What I like about it

This cafe is well known for its great service and friendly staff, that is always ready to help you. Democratic prices are something that should be said about Farini. You will definitely appreciate the cozy atmosphere and homey decor.
